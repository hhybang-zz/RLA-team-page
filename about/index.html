About

The Refugee Learning Accelerator supports engineers and computer scientists from the Middle East to create technologies for refugee learners. We provides access to learning, mentorship, and funding. The Accelerator is a project of the Media Lab Learning Initiative. The Media Lab is an antidisciplinary research lab at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology that designs technologies for people to create a better future.
