Refugee Learning Accelerator

We support engineers and computer scientists from the Middle East to create technology for refugee learners.

We provide training, mentorship and funding to teams through a six-month accelerator program.

Learn more

The Refugee Learning Accelerator is part of the Learning Initiative in the MIT Media Lab.
